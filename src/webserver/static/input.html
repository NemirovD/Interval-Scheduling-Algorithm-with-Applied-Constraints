UOIT Course Optimizer


The UOIT Course Optimizer was created by students, for students as a tool to identify all of the class CRNs corresponding to your ideal school schedule. To create your ideal school schedule, input your semester, courses, and schedule preferences in the fields below, and click "Submit" to see the calendar and CRN list for your preferred school schedule. If you can't remember what courses you have to take, or would like to see what course options are available, please visit the UOIT academic calendar for the correct school year here.
